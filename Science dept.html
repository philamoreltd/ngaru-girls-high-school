Mathematics & Science Department


Mathematics

Mathematics is a core subject that is really the backbone of good performance in a school. For the school to move from a good school to a great school the performance of Mathematics must positively improve. If students improve in Maths they will automatically improve in all other subjects because they have learnt to be patient persistent even when things are not working in their favour.
Our target mean for K.C.S.E this year is 7.0+ and we strongly believe it is achievable.

Chemistry

Chemistry is a universal Science subject that requires universal skills which deals with facts. All Science careers require Chemistry as either the 1st or 2nd subject. Example: Medicine st requires Biology as the 1 subject and Chemistry as the 2nd subject. In Engineering, Physics is the 1st subject, while Chemistry is the 2nd subject. In Pharmacy, Chemistry is the first subject. Science based careers can never be substituted by growing technology.
