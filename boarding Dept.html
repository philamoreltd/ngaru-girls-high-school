Boarding Department

Ngaru Girls' High School is a full girls' boarding high school. 1214 girls are accommodated in the school. The dormitories have spacious and well ventilated cubicles. Here we prioritize cleanliness. The accommodation section is managed by matron and assisted by dormitory mistresses who guide, counsel and discipline the girls in their respective houses.
