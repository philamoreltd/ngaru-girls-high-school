Ngaru Girls Profile


When you come to Ngaru high school,you discover that you have arrived at a special place. The school is set on a pleasant and serene surrounding conducive to academic and intellectual growth.
The school is sponsered by the Catholic dioses of Murang'a and has a legally constituted of management chaired by Mr. Tephen Gatei Kithaka who is the 9th B.O.M chairman since inception. The headship has been stable beacause the current Chief principal is the ninth since the school started in 1982.
On 6th June, 2022, the school was renamed after a Patron saint-St. Catherine of Alexandria, by Rt Rev. James Maria Wainaina- Bishop,Catholic Dioses of murang'a.

Historical background

Ngaru Girls' secondary school opened its doors to its first students on 2nd march,1982 as a mixed day school with an enrollement of 23 students, 16 Boys and 7 Girls. The School went on running as a purely Harambee day school upto 26th may, 1986 when it was taken over as a Government maintained school. It became a purely girls school in 1989.
Since then, the school has grown from a sinle stream school to a threebstreamed school and currently a seven streamed school with a population of about 1,250 students.
